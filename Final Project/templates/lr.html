LR Model Metrics Scores



This Graphs Depicts the actual Values for the Metrics Score gained from the Model from Logistic Regression.


1. Accuracy - Accuracy measures the proportion of true results among the total number of cases examined.
2. Precision - Precision is the ratio of correctly predicted positive observations to the total predicted positives.
3. Recall - Recall is the ratio of correctly predicted positive observations to the all observations in actual class.
4. F1 Score - The F1 score is the weighted average of Precision and Recall.



A confusion matrix is a table used to evaluate the performance of a classification model. It shows the predicted and actual classifications and helps understand the accuracy and error types of the model.
